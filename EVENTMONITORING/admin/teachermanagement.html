Template & Format Guide

Required Columns

  • Employee ID — Unique employee identifier
  • Last Name — Teacher's last name
  • First Name — Teacher's first name
  • Email — Valid school email
  • Department — Grade level or subject department

Optional Columns

  • Middle Name — May be left blank
  • Suffix — e.g. Jr., Sr., III — May be left blank
  • Phone — Contact number

Notes

  • Employee IDs that already exist in the database are skipped
  • Do not delete the header row
  • Remove sample data before importing
